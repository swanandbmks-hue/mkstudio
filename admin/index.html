📖 Admin Panel Guide

How to Upload Images & Videos

Go to Gallery & Media in the sidebar. Drag and drop your image/video files directly onto the upload zone, or click to browse your computer. Files are stored and shown in the Gallery section of your public website.


How to Add a New Production

Go to Productions → click "+ Add New". Fill in the title, year, category (film/short/web/ad), description, YouTube link, and upload a still image. Click "Save & Publish" to make it live.


How to Add Equipment Photos

Go to Equipment → click "Edit" next to any item → scroll to the photo upload area → upload your equipment image. Save changes.


How to Add Client Reviews

Go to Testimonials → click "+ Add New" → fill in the client name, their role, star rating, and review text → click "Save & Publish".


How to Hide/Show Content

Every item has a "Hide" button. Hidden items are not shown to website visitors but are saved. Click "Show" to restore them.


For Production Deployment

To enable server-side saving, add admin/save.php to your hosting server. All content changes will be written to data/content.json automatically. Contact your web developer to set this up on your hosting account.
