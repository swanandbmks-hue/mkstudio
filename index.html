Our Story

Born From a Belief in Cinema's Power

In 2008, visionary filmmaker Machindra Kisan Dhumal founded MK Studio with a conviction that cinema is more than entertainment — it's a force for social awareness, cultural expression, and human connection.

From producing award-worthy Marathi feature films to equipping independent content creators with cinema-grade tools, MK Studio has grown into Pune's most versatile creative hub.
