Our Story

Born From a Belief in Cinema's Power

In 2008, visionary filmmaker Machindra Kisan Dhumal founded MK Studio with a conviction that cinema is more than entertainment — it's a force for social awareness, cultural expression, and human connection.

From producing award-worthy Marathi feature films to equipping independent content creators with cinema-grade tools, MK Studio has grown into Pune's most versatile creative hub. Whether you're shooting your first YouTube series or your tenth feature film, we have the space, gear, and expertise to make it exceptional.
